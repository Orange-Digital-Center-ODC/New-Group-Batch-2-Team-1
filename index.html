FC Fassell — Pride of Monrovia

FC Fassell is a professional football club from Monrovia, Liberia, known for its fast-paced attack, passionate fans, and commitment to youth development. Founded with a vision to raise local talent, FC Fassell competes in national competitions and has become one of Liberia’s most respected clubs. !
