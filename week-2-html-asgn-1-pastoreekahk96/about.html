Brief History Of FC Fassell

FC Fassell, also known as "The Soccer Missionaries," is a Liberian football club based in Paynesville, outside Monrovia. The club was founded with the mission of developing young football talent in Liberia and giving opportunities to local players. Over the years, FC Fassell became known as one of the most organized and ambitious clubs in the country. The team gained promotion to the Liberian Premier League and quickly built a reputation for discipline, teamwork, and a commitment to grassroots football.
